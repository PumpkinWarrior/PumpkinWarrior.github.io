Guidelines

General

  1. Please record on the best way as possible.
    • Minimum amount of Background noise.
    • I highly encourage proper recording equipment but I accept phone recordings as long as its on the best condition. I wont accept room reverbed recordings.
    • If you live on a rural area and doesn't have proper equipment, don't record at night because cicada ruins the mix.
    • Sing on average distance from your microphone to avoid clippings.
  2. Kindly name your files accordingly.
  3. Audio files shall be on .wav or .midi file format
  4. Please contact me immediately if you have changes you want to make.
  5. Payment is via GCash and PayPal only.
  6. The duration will take effect upon receiving and confirmation of 25% downpayment (Will still depend on negotiation).
  7. The final output will be given to you upon full payment. I will send updates upon working on the project from time to time.
  8. Final masters will be released to you on a 48000 HZ Sample rate, 24 bit depth, .wav file format.
  9. I have the right to reject a commission I’m not comfortable working on.
